During the pandemic, in the last 3 years, the whole world stopped tragically.
Some people lost work, other people lost people, many people have blamed the Lockdown psychologically.

I’m Paolo Conte, i started the ASH project as a therapy and design being my daily pill.
ASH lives between the end and a new beginning, between failure and success, out of time and space.
ASH is a state of mind, a scream to the design world.
ASH as a pro surfer surfs between disciplines. It enhances its passions, distinguishing the virtual and digital world from the real and material one, knows how to grasp the priorities and define the details.
ASH transforms memories and experiences into personally expressive projects.
